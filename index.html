Dr. Albert Einstein

I am a theoretical physicist, widely held to be one of the greatest and most influential scientists of all time. Best known for developing the theory of relativity, I have also made important contributions to quantum mechanics, and am a central figure in the revolutionary reshaping of the scientific understanding of nature that modern physics accomplished in the first decades of the twentieth century.
